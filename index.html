About Me

For nearly ten years I had been pursuing a career as a college professor. During that time the educational tools that I gravitated toward in order to facilitate student engagement ultimately led me down new avenues of learning as well. These opportunities allowed me to explore a more fulfilling role; one which lay in the development and implementation of tools and technologies that may make everyday tasks a little bit easier and a little bit better for everyone. After making the decision to change careers I applied and was accepted to Fullstack Academy’s Flex Immersive bootcamp, which I completed this past December.
